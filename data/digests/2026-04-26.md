- Fidesz faces leadership vacuum and institutional disarray, with the NYT reporting the party is 'licking its wounds' amid restructuring uncertainty.
- Orbán's flagship think-tank is collapsing without state financial backing, revealing his political infrastructure was built on institutional capture, not independent organisational strength.
- Reports from Le Monde and Balkan Insight indicate Orbán may be considering US exile, though no departure has been confirmed.
