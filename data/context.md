## Political situation

DW (Apr 23) identifies three structural obstacles facing Magyar: ECJ LGBTQ ruling, entrenched Orbán-era institutions, and EU fund conditionality. Gáspár Orbán discharged from military (Euronews, Apr 22). Sports policy recalibration expected after Orbán-era football investment.
