Magyar's legislative response and repeal timeline remain entirely unreported. Magyar renewed calls for president and top judges to resign (Reuters, Apr 20). State media broadcast suspension announced alongside press freedom guarantees (Reuters, Apr 15). Politico Europe cautions against scapegoating Orbán-era EU officials during transition — a rule-of-law warning directed at Magyar's own administration. WSJ opinion argues democratic institutions remain structurally weakened despite the transition.
